About

KSEEB stands for Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board. It was established in 1966, and is responsible for providing quality education to the students studying in the affiliated schools. The Board is also responsible for conducting examinations for Class 10 and other examinations like Karnataka open school, the Diploma in Education, Music etc. The Board is headquartered in Malleshwaram, Bangalore.
